Three routes, one product spine.

These are not clones of Apple, CBTL, or Agoda. They are translated ingredients: restraint, warmth, and travel utility.

Apple restraint

Kampung Quiet Ledger

Best for owner onboarding, dashboard calm, and trust. This route makes MyHomestay feel like a clean online register rather than another noisy travel site.

Risk: too quiet if the public listing page does not get enough photo and copy warmth.

Cafe warmth

Rumah Artisan

Best for hosted feeling, owner profile cards, and premium identity later. It adds craft without falling into rustic decoration.

Risk: too beige if we let warmth overpower utility, green, white space, and sharp hierarchy.

Travel utility

Stayflow Malaysia

Best for search, filters, listing comparison, and regional scale. It keeps the directory useful without making the MVP too marketplace-heavy.

Risk: too generic if owner dignity and local trust details do not lead the product voice.
