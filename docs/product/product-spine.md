# MyHomestay Product Spine

## One-Line Positioning

MyHomestay is a clean, trusted homestay directory and owner toolkit for Malaysia's local stay operators, starting with kampung-modern destinations and expanding across Southeast Asia later.

## Core Thesis

Homestay owners in Malaysia often depend on scattered channels: WhatsApp, Facebook, Google Maps, TikTok, and informal referrals. Customers want trust and local context before contacting an owner. Owners need a simple way to look credible, publish listings, and handle inquiries without learning a complex marketplace system.

MyHomestay should win by helping owners create trustworthy listings quickly, then route interested guests straight to WhatsApp for booking and payment during the MVP.

## First Customer

Homestay owners and small local operators in Malaysia.

The first product should optimize for owner onboarding before traveller marketplace scale.

## First User Promise

An owner can create a trustworthy homestay listing in under 10 minutes and share a clean public profile link anywhere they already find customers.

## MVP User Flow

1. Owner signs up.
2. Owner creates a property listing.
3. Owner adds photos, location area, amenities, Muslim-friendly details, house rules, pricing guide, and WhatsApp contact.
4. MyHomestay generates a clean public listing page.
5. Customer views the listing and taps WhatsApp to book or pay directly with the owner.
6. Owner sees a simple dashboard with listing status and basic performance signals.

## Free MVP Scope

- Owner account
- 1 to 3 listings per owner
- Public listing page
- WhatsApp booking CTA
- Basic owner dashboard
- Basic trust/profile fields
- Malaysia-first location structure
- Muslim-friendly and owner-defined filters

## Later Paid Subscription Ideas

- Featured listings
- Verified online owner profile card
- Booking calendar and management
- Monthly income tracker
- Better analytics
- Multi-property tools
- AI listing helper
- Custom mini-site or branded owner page

## Required Listing Fields For MVP

- Homestay name
- Area, town, and state
- WhatsApp number
- Starting price or price range
- Guest capacity
- Bedroom and bathroom count
- At least 3 photos
- Muslim-friendly and family-friendly flags
- Basic amenities
- House rules
- Owner or admin display name

## Brand Feel

- Minimal
- Artisan
- Kampung modern
- Local trust
- Smooth owner flow
- Easy customer discovery

Avoid a generic travel template or direct Airbnb clone.
